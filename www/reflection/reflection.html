About this report

When I set out to make this report, I decided not to do it the usual way of showing images and writing descriptions about them. Instead, I am presenting what I did during my internship quite literally. During work hours, I tracked the titles of windows that were open on my computer. The landing-page of this report is the visualization of that data. I also tracked my activity by collecting a screenshot of my computer every 6 seconds. A selection of these can be seen over here. I felt that this would be a better representation of the work I did at LustLab: telling stories through data and interfaces.
